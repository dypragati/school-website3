About Us

The quest of child education society, the apex body of Bal Bharati Public Scools for 'Better and Better' and a strong commitment to providing quality education to the masses worked as catalysts in conceiving and establishing and eductional institution in Manesar, Haryana.

20703 sq. m of land was aquired in an open auction from HSIIDC- the hayana state industrial infrastructure Development Corporation Ltd.
