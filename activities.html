Recent Activities

    Republic day celebrations.

Republic Day was celebrated on 24 january in Bal Bharati public School, Manesar with a feeling of patriotism in the hearts of all of the students and teachers present there.A beautiful song was presented by the students followed by a speech. A marvellous dance performance was also given by the students of middle section.

    Visit to NGO 'Arvindam' Foundation.

On 18 january 2020, 14 students of Bal bharati Public school visited an NGO 'Arvindam Foundation in Pachgaon under 'Interact Club ' activity. The foundation works to strengthen women by giving them identity as informal workers. Empowerment of women and girls from poor And marginalised communities.

    Inter school Mun.

Students of bal Bharati Public School, Manesar participated in Inter school MUN held on march !9, 2020 at BBPS GRH marg. They participated with great enthusiasm and zeal. Soumya Vats of class XI bagged High commendation award.
